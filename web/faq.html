General Questions

ItoMcovertor is a free, fast, and accurate unit converter that helps you convert between Imperial and Metric measurement systems. It's available as a web app, browser extension, and command-line tool, supporting over 120 different unit conversions across 12 categories.
Yes, ItoMcovertor is completely free to use. There are no premium features, subscriptions, or hidden costs. All conversions, history features, and the browser extension are available at no charge.
ItoMcovertor supports conversions across 12 categories:
  • Length - inches, feet, yards, miles, millimeters, centimeters, meters, kilometers + full SI scale
  • Area - square inches, square feet, acres, square miles, cm2, m2, hectares, km2
  • Volume (Liquid) - teaspoons, tablespoons, cups, pints, quarts, gallons, milliliters, liters
  • Volume (Solid) - cubic inches, cubic feet, cubic yards, cm3, m3
  • Mass - ounces, pounds, stone, tons, grams, kilograms, tonnes
  • Temperature - Fahrenheit, Celsius, Kelvin
  • Speed - mph, ft/s, km/h, m/s
  • Pressure - psi, inHg, Pa, kPa, bar
  • Energy - BTU, ft-lb, Joules, kilojoules
  • Power - horsepower, Watts, kilowatts
  • Force - pound-force, Newtons
  • Nautical - nautical miles, kilometers
ItoMcovertor uses precise conversion factors based on international standards. Results are displayed with up to 6 decimal places for maximum accuracy. For scientific or engineering applications, our conversions meet professional accuracy requirements.

Browser Extension

The ItoMcovertor extension is available for Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox support is also available. You can install it from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons.
After installing, simply highlight any measurement text on a webpage (like "5 miles" or "100 kg"). The extension will automatically detect the unit and show a tooltip with the converted value. You can also click the extension icon to open the popup converter for manual conversions.
No. The extension works entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All conversions happen locally in your browser. Your settings and history are stored only on your device. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Features & Settings

Yes! When "Auto-save History" is enabled in settings, your recent conversions are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. You can view, copy, or clear your history at any time. History is stored only on your device and never sent to any server.
