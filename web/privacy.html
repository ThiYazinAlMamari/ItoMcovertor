Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

ItoMcovertor is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our unit converter website and Chrome extension handle your data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

All data stays on your device:

  • Conversion History: Stored locally in your browser's localStorage (only with your consent)
  • Settings: Theme preference and display options stored locally (only with your consent)
  • Cookie Consent: We store a small cookie to remember your privacy preference
  • No Analytics: We do not use any tracking or analytics services
  • No External Data: No data is ever sent to our servers or third parties

Cookie Policy

We use minimal cookies to provide you with the best experience. Here's what you need to know:

What Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
unitConverter_cookieConsent Remembers your cookie preference (accepted/declined) 1 year

Local Storage (With Your Consent)

If you accept cookies, we also use browser localStorage to save:

  • unitConverter_settings: Your display preferences (theme, decimal places, etc.)
  • unitConverter_history: Your recent conversion history (up to 10 entries)

Your Choices

  • Accept: Your settings and history will be saved for future visits
  • Decline: No data will be stored. The site works normally, but settings reset on each visit
  • Change Your Mind: Clear your browser cookies/data to reset your choice and see the banner again

How to Delete Cookies

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data

Chrome Extension Permissions

The Chrome extension requests minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings and history locally
  • Active Tab (optional): Only when using auto-convert feature to read selected text

The extension does not send any data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: To load the Rubik font family. Google may collect anonymous usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your data at any time by:

  • Clicking "Clear" in the conversion history panel
  • Clearing your browser's localStorage for this site
  • Uninstalling the Chrome extension

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
