1. Introduction and Overview

ItoMcovertor is a free, open-source unit conversion tool designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe that users should be able to perform unit conversions without sacrificing their privacy or being tracked across the internet. This commitment to privacy is reflected in every aspect of how we design and operate our Services.

Unlike many online tools and services, ItoMcovertor does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information about its users. All conversion calculations are performed entirely within your web browser or browser extension, meaning your data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

This Privacy Policy is intended to provide you with complete transparency about our data practices. We want you to understand exactly what information, if any, is collected when you use our Services, how that information is used, and what choices you have regarding your information.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

While we do not collect any information on our servers, our Services may store certain information locally on your device to enhance your user experience. This information is stored using your browser's built-in storage mechanisms (localStorage and cookies) and never leaves your device. You have complete control over this locally stored information and can delete it at any time.

3.1 Cookie Consent Preference

When you first visit our Website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner asking whether you wish to allow us to store data locally on your device. Your response to this consent request is stored in a cookie on your device so that we do not need to ask you again on subsequent visits. This cookie is named "unitConverter_cookieConsent" and contains only your consent preference (accepted or declined). This cookie expires after one (1) year from the date it is set.

3.2 User Preferences and Settings

If you consent to local storage, we may store your user preferences and settings in your browser's localStorage. These preferences may include, but are not limited to:

  • Theme Preference: Whether you prefer the light theme or dark theme for the user interface.
  • Decimal Places: Your preferred number of decimal places for conversion results.
  • Scientific Notation: Whether you prefer results to be displayed in scientific notation.
  • Default Units: Your preferred default units for various conversion categories.
  • Display Options: Various display preferences such as showing or hiding certain UI elements.

This information is stored in a localStorage item named "unitConverter_settings" and remains on your device until you clear your browser data or explicitly delete it through our interface.

3.3 Conversion History

If you consent to local storage, our Services may store a limited history of your recent conversions to allow you to quickly access and repeat previous calculations. This history is stored locally on your device and typically includes:

  • The source unit and target unit of the conversion
  • The input value and resulting output value
  • The timestamp of when the conversion was performed

This conversion history is stored in a localStorage item named "unitConverter_history" and is limited to the most recent ten (10) conversions. Older entries are automatically removed when new conversions are added. You can clear this history at any time by clicking the "Clear" button in the conversion history panel or by clearing your browser's localStorage for our domain.

3.4 Your Control Over Local Storage

You have complete control over the information stored locally on your device. You can:

  • Decline Cookies: When presented with the cookie consent banner, you can choose to decline, and no data will be stored on your device. The Services will continue to function normally, but your preferences will not be saved between visits.
  • Clear Conversion History: You can clear your conversion history at any time by clicking the "Clear" button in the history panel.
  • Reset All Settings: You can reset all settings to their default values through the Settings panel.
  • Clear Browser Data: You can use your browser's built-in tools to clear cookies and localStorage for our Website, which will remove all locally stored data.
  • Use Private/Incognito Mode: Using your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent any data from being stored persistently on your device.

4. Browser Extension Permissions and Data Practices

The ItoMcovertor browser extension is designed with the same privacy-first principles as our Website. The extension requests minimal permissions necessary to provide its functionality:

4.1 Storage Permission

The extension requests the "storage" permission to save your user preferences (such as theme preference and display options) locally within the extension's storage area. This storage is isolated to the extension and is not accessible by websites or other extensions. The data stored includes the same types of preferences described in Section 3.2 above. This storage is synced across your browsers if you are signed into your browser account with sync enabled.

4.2 Active Tab Permission (Optional)

The extension may request the "activeTab" permission, which is only used when you explicitly activate the auto-convert feature. This permission allows the extension to read text that you have selected on the current webpage so that it can identify numbers with units and offer to convert them. This permission is considered optional because:

  • It is only activated when you explicitly request it (e.g., by clicking the extension icon or using a keyboard shortcut)
  • It only has access to the currently active tab, not your browsing history or other tabs
  • It only reads the selected text, not the entire page content
  • The text is processed entirely locally and is never transmitted to any servers

4.3 No Background Data Collection

The extension does not run any background processes that collect data. It does not monitor your browsing activity, track the websites you visit, record your keystrokes, or perform any other form of surveillance. The extension only becomes active when you explicitly interact with it.

4.4 No Network Requests

The extension does not make any network requests to external servers. All conversion calculations are performed locally using the mathematical formulas built into the extension. The extension does not phone home, check for updates through its own mechanisms (browser-managed updates only), or transmit any data whatsoever.
