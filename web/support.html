Top Questions

What is ItoMcovertor?

A free, fast unit converter for Imperial & Metric. Available as web app, browser extension, and CLI with 120+ conversions.

How do I use the extension?

Highlight any measurement on a webpage (like "5 miles"). The extension auto-detects and shows a conversion tooltip.

What units can I convert?

Length, Area, Volume, Mass, Temperature, Speed, Pressure, Energy, Power, Force & Nautical — 12 categories total.

Does it work offline? Is it private?

Yes! Extension works 100% offline. No data collected — everything runs locally in your browser.
