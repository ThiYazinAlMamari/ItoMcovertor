Troubleshooting

Conversions not saving?

Make sure "Auto-save History" is enabled in Settings. Also check that your browser allows localStorage for this site.

Theme not persisting?

Your theme preference is saved in localStorage. If it resets, your browser may be clearing site data on close. Check your privacy settings.

Extension not working?

Try disabling and re-enabling the extension. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall from the Chrome Web Store.
